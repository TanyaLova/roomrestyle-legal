Privacy Policy

Effective June 14, 2026

1. Information We Handle

RoomRestyle AI may handle photos you upload or capture; prompts, notes, style selections, design choices, and reference images; generated previews and saved project details; app language, subscription status, purchase confirmation, and credit usage; basic device, app version, diagnostics, crash, and usage information needed to operate and improve the app; and contact or support information you provide when requesting help.

2. Photos and Media

RoomRestyle AI processes only photos and images you choose to capture, select, upload, or use as references. Granting camera or photo permission does not mean the app automatically accesses your entire photo library. Avoid uploading images that show faces, addresses, documents, children, or other private details unless necessary and you have the appropriate permission.

3. How Uploaded Photos Are Used

Uploaded photos, reference images, prompts, and design choices are used to provide AI design previews, display projects and references, support edits or regeneration, and maintain app functionality. When production AI features are connected, photos and prompts may be transmitted to service providers that process them on our behalf. AI previews are for inspiration and visual preview only, not professional architectural, construction, engineering, or safety advice.

4. AI Processing and Service Providers

When you use AI design features, uploaded photos, reference images, masks, prompts, notes, and design selections may be transmitted to third-party AI, cloud storage, analytics, crash reporting, and infrastructure providers that process this information on our behalf. These providers are used as needed to operate the app, generate previews, troubleshoot issues, protect the service, and deliver requested results.

RoomRestyle AI does not sell uploaded photos or generated results. Generated previews are temporary service outputs and may be stored only as needed to process your request, display recent results, and support app functionality, subject to the retention limits described in this policy.

5. How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features; process AI generation and editing requests; save and display projects; manage credits, purchases, and subscription status; respond to support requests; improve performance and troubleshoot issues; prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access; protect users and the service; and comply with legal obligations.

6. Sharing

We do not sell uploaded photos, and we do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with service providers that support AI processing, storage, analytics, crash reporting, customer support, subscription and payment processing, security, and other app operations. Information may also be disclosed when required by law, to respond to valid legal requests, or when reasonably needed to protect users, rights, safety, and service security.

7. Payments and Subscriptions

Purchases may be processed by the Apple App Store, Google Play, or another authorized payment provider. RoomRestyle AI may receive subscription status, purchase confirmation, renewal or cancellation status, product identifiers, and related transaction metadata needed to provide paid features and restore purchases. RoomRestyle AI does not store full payment card details.

8. Storage and Retention

Some uploaded images, generated previews, and project details may be stored locally on your device so projects remain available. Cloud or server retention will depend on the production AI, storage, support, and operational providers used by the app. Information is retained only as reasonably needed to provide the service, maintain records, prevent fraud or abuse, meet legal obligations, enforce agreements, or resolve security and technical issues. Uploaded photos, reference images, masks, and generated results are temporarily stored in our cloud storage only as needed to process your request and deliver your result. These files are automatically scheduled for deletion from active cloud storage within 24–48 hours.

9. User Choices

You choose which photos and references to upload. You can remove saved projects where the app provides that feature, manage camera and photo permissions in device settings, and manage or cancel subscriptions through the applicable app store. You may use the Contact Support option in the Account screen for privacy or data requests.

10. Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or information about certain personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing where applicable. California, the European Union, the United Kingdom, and other regions may provide additional rights. Rights vary, may be subject to legal exceptions, and will be supported where applicable to RoomRestyle AI and the information it controls.

11. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. However, no storage, device, network, or transmission method is completely secure, and RoomRestyle AI cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children's Privacy

RoomRestyle AI is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum legal age in their country. Do not upload photos of children unless you have appropriate permission and a legitimate reason to use the image. If you believe a child provided personal information improperly, use the Contact Support option in the Account screen.

13. International Data Transfers

Information may be processed or stored in countries other than the country where you live, including where service providers operate. Privacy laws in those countries may differ. Where required, reasonable safeguards may be used for international transfers.

14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as RoomRestyle AI features, production providers, legal requirements, and data practices change. The effective date will be revised when updates are made, and additional notice may be provided where required.

15. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, use the Contact Support option in the Account screen.
